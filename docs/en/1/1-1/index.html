1.1 Warning

Mark Warning
In case of malfunction or abnormality, immediately turn off the power and discontinue use.
・When smoke or abnormal smells or noises are emitted
・When water or foreign matter adheres to the product
・When the product is damagedd
Do not disassemble or modify the product. Failure to do so may result in product failure, fire, etc.
Do not use the product with a power supply voltage other than the specified voltage.
Do not touch the product with wet hands or disconnectd the power cable.
Do not use the product in a watery place where them isa lot of moisture, humidity steam, or dust.
